Step 6: Getting reviews and getting paid

In this step, we'll give you an opportunity to have your current clients review your work as well as friends that can vouch for you. We'll also have you tell us how you prefer to get paid. Watch the short video to help you complete this step.

In this step, you need to let us know how you prefer to get paid. If you feel more comfortable leaving this step until later, we're completely fine with that. You will, however, need to complete it before you perform your first job. This video explains how the process works. The biggest thing to remember is you never pay anything to obtain your clients. You do pay for the cost of processing the payment from the customer, usually 2.5%. You can consider this your cost of doing business. We will never charge you more than it costs us, and we will continue to try and bring this cost down.
